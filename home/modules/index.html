Modules in SmartTraining

Differents modules to variate analysis.

Statistic and Features Analysis

Techniques such as PCA, correlations, spatial deformations, mutual information, etc., are implemented to evaluate the features in the data set. Additionally, statistical evaluations will be implemented to evaluate normality, outliers, dispersion, frequency, etc.

Clustering Data

K-Means, Mean Shift, DBScan, Hierarchical and Agglomerative clustering, Affinity Propagation, are the main algorithms implemented to generate divisions in the data set, find patterns or associations of elements given the data that represents them. Being evaluated by different interest metrics.

Classifiers and Predictors

Training of classification and prediction models can be carried out through the implementation of different supervised learning algorithms. Different parameters are considered and responses are evaluated according to the type of model to be used. Considering overfitting and dimensionality problems.
