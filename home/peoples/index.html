About Us

Members of team and detail financing for project development.

Members

The members belong to CeBiB, University of Chile or Andres Bello University, it is a mutidisciplinary team, related both to data science, software engineering and the development of models based on artificial intelligence and mathematical modeling of complex systems.

Instituion and Afiliation

Centre for Biotechnology and Bioengineering, University of Chile and Andres Bello University, are institutions that take part of this project, working in collaboration mode, thus increasing the development of multidisciplinary tools.

Financing

The project has been financed mainly by Fondecyt project 1141311 (Conicyt, Chile) and the Centre for Biotechnology and Bioengineering – CeBiB (PIA project FB0001, Conicyt, Chile).
